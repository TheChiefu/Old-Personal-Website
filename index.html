What is the purpose of this website?

I created this page as both a digital portfolio of myself but also as a database of my personal information and interests over time. I like making digital "time-capsules" of my time being on the internet; my YouTube channel being an example. I started this trend after watching some of my old YouTube videos from a couple of years back and I noticed how much not only my voiced changed over-time but my sense in vocabulary and communication. I've always been interested in documenting my life, my grandmother who loved to take photos gave me the inspiration of wanting to document my life. Unluckily I haven't had access to younger photos of myself when I was younger but considering I've been using the internet since I was around 7 years of age; and whatever you put on the internet never goes away and neither did anything I've ever posted. So this website is dedicated to not only my history but my life in general.

How often will the website update?

A problem I will admit is that when it comes to updating anything online I am terrible at remembering to do so. I want to remember to update the website with the newest and most relevant information I can; but if anything doesn't seem urgent or important enough to attend to online I most likely won't do it. I generally focus on doing school work and more real-life situations first than update something online that, although I do care about don't find necessary or urgent enough to update.
TL;DR - The majority of the time no, but if I remember I will.
